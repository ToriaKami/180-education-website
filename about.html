The Architect of Opportunity.

My name is Harper, and I believe that talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. My mission is to change that.

My Story

As a freelance advocate, I've dedicated my career to seeking out companies interested in giving back through education. I don't just find funding; I build flexible, purpose-driven partnerships that create meaningful scholarship opportunities.

My Approach

I act as the bridge between corporate generosity and student ambition. I work with businesses to design impactful scholarship programs, then connect those programs with universities, colleges, and the students who need them most. My goal is simple: to unlock access to books, tutoring, materials, and tuition so that more students can thrive.
